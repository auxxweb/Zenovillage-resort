Amenities at Zeno Village

Home Amenities

Spacious Lodging for Memorable Gatherings

We offer spacious lodging options to host large gatherings of up to 150 people. Ensuring everyone experiences the charm of Zeno Village.

Balcony Views and Cozy Comfort Await

Select from our 10 rooms, with 5 offering breathtaking balconies for an immersive natural experience and 6 providing snug and inviting retreats.

WiFi Facilities

Stay connected to the world while you unwind in the midst of nature. Zeno Village proudly introduces WiFi to 900 Kandi, making us the pioneers of connectivity in this tranquil haven.

Culinary Bliss Amidst Nature's Beauty

Discover Culinary Delights at Zeno Village's Dining Area. Experience a harmonious blend of local flavors and global cuisines in a serene natural setting. Our restaurant offers an inviting atmosphere where you can savor exquisite dishes prepared with care and passion, making each meal a delightful journey.

Comfort and Breakfast: The Perfect CP Combination

Choose the CP Plan (Continental Plan) for your stay at Zeno Village.This package includes comfortable accommodations and a scrumptious continental breakfast that sets the tone for your day of exploration and relaxation. Enjoy a nourishing start to your mornings amidst the tranquil beauty of 900 Kandi.

Diverse Flavors, One Experience

Indulge in the Buffet Plan at Zeno Village, a culinary experience that brings together a diverse array of flavors. Immerse yourself in an unlimited buffet spread, featuring an assortment of cuisines and delectable dishes. It's the perfect option for those who appreciate variety and a hearty meal during their stay.

Happiness and Peace of Mind

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of our natural ambience, fostering happiness and peace of mind throughout your stay.

Natural Ambience

Our resort is enveloped by the untouched beauty of nature, providing an authentic and rejuvenating experience for our guests.

Trekking

Explore the untouched beauty of 900 Kandi through thrilling treks. Traverse winding trails, encounter wildlife, and witness breathtaking vistas on guided trekking adventures.

Off-road Jeep Drive

Experience off-road excitement as you embark on rugged jeep drives through the wilderness. Conquer challenging terrains and soak in the rugged natural beauty.

Waterfall

Discover nature's wonders with visits to captivating waterfalls. Immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of cascading waters, a rejuvenating treat for the senses.

DJ

Get ready to groove under the stars with our DJ nights. Dance to lively beats, enjoy great music, and create unforgettable memories in our vibrant atmosphere.

Campfire

Gather around the campfire and share stories under the starlit sky. Experience the warmth and camaraderie that comes from spending evenings by the fire.

Meet-ups

Connect with fellow travelers through our social meet-ups. Join gatherings, make new friends, and share experiences that will enrich your stay.

Events

Experience memorable musical events at Zeno Village. Our event spaces set the stage for music-filled gatherings, concerts, and entertainment that will leave you dancing under the stars.

Workstation

Combine work and relaxation with our serene workstation. Enjoy the tranquility of nature while staying productive in our comfortable remote office setup.
