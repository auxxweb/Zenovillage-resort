Welcome to Zenovillage

Our Zenovillage resort in Wayanad offers a peaceful forest resort nestled in the heart of Wayanad, where the beauty of nature meets the comfort of home.Surrounded by towering trees, lush greenery, and the soothing sounds of the forest, we invite you to experience a unique blend of relaxation, adventure, and eco-conscious living.

At Zenovillage, we believe in offering more than just a place to stay.We provide an opportunity to see nature in its most pure nature and to escape from the fast-paced world. Whether you're here for a peaceful getaway, an outdoor adventure, or to discover Wayanad's diverse culture and wildlife, Zenovillage is the ideal place to rejuvenate your body, mind, and soul.

Our Mission

At Zeno Village, our mission is to provide unparalleled experiences that seamlessly blend luxury, nature, and sustainability. We are committed to creating lasting memories for our guests by offering exceptional hospitality in an eco-conscious environment. Through innovation and a dedication to preserving the beauty of 900 Kandi, we aim to be a beacon of responsible tourism while crafting unique stories for every guest.

Our vision at Zeno Village is to be the epitome of eco-friendly luxury in 900 Kandi. We aspire to set new standards in sustainable hospitality by continuously minimizing our environmental footprint while maximizing guest satisfaction. We envision a future where our resort stands as a testament to the perfect harmony between nature and modern comfort, inspiring others to follow our lead in preserving the natural beauty of this region.
